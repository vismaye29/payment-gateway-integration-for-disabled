About Us

Donors Help To Live their Dreams and Make a Happy Living

We are a premier multi-speciality care centre of disabled people of India and have been acknowledged globally as the centre of care and nurture. Over the years, Samaradhana Care centre has developed unmatched trust with its patients on the basis of a strong foundation which includes the state-of-the-art facilities, best medical expertise, research, education and charitable endeavours. We are extremely proud that today, we serve patients from all walks of life. We believe in ‘Sarvetra Sukhinah:Santu, Sarve Santu Niramaya:’ which means ‘Let all be blissful, Let all stay healthy’. We believe that "EVERY PERSON WITH A DISABILITY IS AN INDIVIDUAL HIMSELF".
